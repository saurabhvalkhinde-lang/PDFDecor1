GST Invoice Format as per Indian Law

Under the Goods and Services Tax (GST) regime, every registered business in India must issue a proper tax invoice. PDFDecor's invoice generator includes all mandatory fields required by the GST Act:

  • Supplier's name, address, and GSTIN
  • Recipient's name, address, and GSTIN (if registered)
  • Unique invoice number and date
  • Description, quantity, unit, and value of goods/services
  • Taxable value, CGST/SGST/IGST rates and amounts
  • HSN/SAC code support
