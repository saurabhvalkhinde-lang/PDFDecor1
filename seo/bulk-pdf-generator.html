Frequently Asked Questions

How many PDFs can I generate at once?

Up to 500 records per batch. For larger volumes, split your CSV into batches of 500 and run multiple sessions.

What CSV format do I need?

UTF-8 encoded CSV with a header row. Each document type has its own column structure. Click "Download CSV Template" to get the exact format for your chosen document type.

How long does it take to generate 100 PDFs?

On a modern laptop or desktop, 100 PDFs typically take 2–4 minutes. Processing speed depends on your device's performance. A progress bar shows real-time status.

Can I retry failed PDFs?

Yes. After generation completes, any failed rows show a "Retry" option. You can re-process only the failed records without regenerating the successful ones.

What if the ZIP is empty or some PDFs fail?

Try reducing batch size to 50–100 rows at a time. Use a modern browser on desktop (Chrome or Edge recommended). Mobile browsers may struggle with large batches.

Is it completely free with no limits?

Yes — completely free. No per-PDF charges, no subscription. The service is ad-supported. All PDFs include a small "PDFDecor.in" footer watermark.
