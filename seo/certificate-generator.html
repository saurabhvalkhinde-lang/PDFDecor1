Frequently Asked Questions

Is the certificate generator completely free?

Yes — 100% free. All templates, bulk generation, and PDF downloads are free. The PDF includes a small "PDFDecor.in" footer. No subscription, no credit card needed.

Can I add my school/company logo?

Yes! You can add a logo URL in the certificate form. The logo appears at the top of the certificate. Logo upload from local file is coming soon.

Can I generate certificates for 200 students at once?

Absolutely. Use the Bulk Certificate Generator — upload a CSV with all student names and course names, and generate all 200 certificates as individual PDFs, then download them as a single ZIP file.

Are the certificates print-ready?

Yes. All certificates are generated in A4 format (210×297mm) at high resolution, suitable for printing and digital sharing via WhatsApp, email, or social media.

Can I add a signature or stamp?

Currently you can add the authorised signatory name and designation. Image-based signature upload is on our roadmap for a future update.

Is my data stored on any server?

No. Everything is processed in your browser only. No student names, org details, or any data is ever uploaded or stored on any server.
