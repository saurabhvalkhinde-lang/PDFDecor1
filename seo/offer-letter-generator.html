Standard Offer Letter Format for India

An employment offer letter in India typically includes:

  • Company letterhead with name and address
  • Candidate's full name and address
  • Position/designation being offered
  • Department and reporting manager
  • CTC (Cost to Company) or gross salary
  • Expected date of joining
  • Probation period details
  • Company signature and candidate acceptance section
